# RoomBud - Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

RoomBud is a community-driven platform where users connect to find roommates and housing arrangements. To maintain a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment for all users, we have established these Community Guidelines. All users agree to follow these guidelines when using RoomBud.

Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, content removal, account suspension, or permanent banning.

---

## 2. Core Values

RoomBud is built on the following principles:

- **Respect:** Treat all community members with dignity and respect
- **Honesty:** Provide truthful, accurate information in profiles and communications
- **Safety:** Prioritize the safety and well-being of all users
- **Inclusivity:** Welcome users from all backgrounds, identities, and experiences
- **Accountability:** Take responsibility for your actions in the community

---

## 3. Prohibited Content and Behavior

### 3.1 Discrimination and Hate Speech

**Prohibited:**
- Discriminating against or harassing users based on:
  - Race, ethnicity, or national origin
  - Gender identity or sexual orientation
  - Religion or spiritual beliefs
  - Disability status or health conditions
  - Age
  - Socioeconomic status
  - Any other protected characteristic
- Promoting discrimination, violence, or hate toward any group
- Dehumanizing language or slurs
- Conspiracy theories tied to protected groups
- Promoting fascism, supremacist ideologies, or hate movements

**Examples of Violations:**
- "I won't live with [group]. They're all [stereotype]"
- Slurs or derogatory terms for any group
- Promoting extremist ideologies
- Coded language meant to exclude or demean

**Consequences:** Immediate account suspension or permanent ban

### 3.2 Harassment and Bullying

**Prohibited:**
- Targeted harassment of individual users
- Repeated, unwanted contact after being told to stop
- Cyberbullying or public humiliation
- Threats of violence or harm
- Doxxing (sharing private information to harass)
- Coordinated harassment campaigns

**Examples of Violations:**
- Sending repeated unwanted messages to a user who blocked you
- Creating fake profiles to harass or mock someone
- Publicly sharing someone's personal information to shame them
- Threatening messages or comments

**Consequences:** Warning, account suspension, or permanent ban

### 3.3 Sexual Harassment and Exploitation

**Prohibited:**
- Unwanted sexual advances or comments
- Requesting sexual favors or services
- Sharing sexual content without consent
- Sexual coercion or quid pro quo offers
- Exploitation or abuse

**Examples of Violations:**
- Unsolicited sexual messages or comments
- "If you room with me, I expect sexual access"
- Sharing intimate photos without consent
- Using the platform to solicit sex work

**Consequences:** Immediate account suspension or permanent ban

### 3.4 Minors on Platform

**Strictly Prohibited:**
- Any account created by or for users under 18 years old
- Attempting to connect with minors through the platform
- Soliciting contact from underage users
- Any form of child endangerment

**RoomBud's Response:** Immediate permanent ban, law enforcement notification, and cooperation with authorities to prevent child exploitation

### 3.5 Misinformation and Fraud

**Prohibited:**
- Fake profiles or identity fraud
- Misrepresenting property details (size, condition, location)
- Inflating or deflating rental prices deceptively
- False claims about amenities or features
- Scams or financial fraud
- Fake rental listings
- Catfishing

**Examples of Violations:**
- Posting someone else's photos as your own
- Claiming a property is in a city where it isn't
- Hiding structural damage or pest infestations
- Bait-and-switch pricing schemes

**Consequences:** Content removal, account warning, suspension, or ban

### 3.6 Illegal Activities and Content

**Prohibited:**
- Using the platform to facilitate illegal activities:
  - Drug trafficking
  - Weapons sales
  - Human trafficking
  - Prostitution or sex trafficking
  - Money laundering
  - Fraud or theft
- Sharing illegal content (stolen goods, hacked information)
- Promoting or facilitating violence
- Terrorism or violent extremism

**Consequences:** Permanent ban and legal reporting to authorities

### 3.7 Offensive Language and Content

**Prohibited:**
- Excessive profanity in profiles or messages
- Vulgar or sexually explicit content
- Graphic violence or disturbing imagery
- Hate speech or slurs
- Glorification of violence or self-harm

**Examples of Violations:**
- Extremely vulgar bio descriptions
- Graphic violent imagery in profile photos
- Gratuitous use of slurs or offensive language

**Consequences:** Content warning, removal, or account suspension

### 3.8 Spam and Manipulation

**Prohibited:**
- Unsolicited commercial promotion or advertising
- Repetitive messaging or "spamming"
- Automated bots or fake accounts
- Manipulating the algorithm through coordinated fake activity
- Fake reviews or ratings
- Link spam or phishing attempts

**Examples of Violations:**
- Sending the same message to hundreds of users
- Multiple fake profiles voting on your real profile
- Posting links to external money-making schemes
- Phishing links disguised as housing listings

**Consequences:** Content removal, account warning, or suspension

### 3.9 Privacy Violations

**Prohibited:**
- Sharing other users' personal information without consent (doxxing)
- Recording or screenshotting private conversations to share publicly
- Sharing someone's location information
- Publishing contact details without permission
- Screenshots of private messages to shame or embarrass

**Consequences:** Account warning, suspension, or permanent ban

### 3.10 Impersonation

**Prohibited:**
- Pretending to be another person
- Creating accounts in someone else's name
- Impersonating RoomBud staff or moderators
- Falsely claiming official status or endorsement

**Consequences:** Account suspension or permanent ban

## 4. Profile and Content Standards

### 4.1 Profile Requirements

**Acceptable:**
- Authentic and recent profile photos
- Photos that accurately represent the user
- Accurate personal information (age, gender, profession)
- Honest description of your preferences and lifestyle
- Genuine budget and location information

**Unacceptable:**
- Completely obscured or illegible photos
- Photos of others, celebrities, or objects instead of you
- Heavily filtered or altered photos that misrepresent appearance
- Pet photos, cartoon characters, or memes

### 4.2 Profile Descriptions

**Acceptable:**
- Honest description of lifestyle and preferences
- Information about your interests and hobbies
- Clear description of what you're looking for in a roommate
- Respectful mention of preferences

**Unacceptable:**
- Discriminatory statements
- Extreme vulgarity
- Hateful or dehumanizing language
- Requests for illegal services
- Contact information (phone, email, external social media)
- Promotional content or links to external sites

### 4.3 Images

**Acceptable:**
- Clear, recent photos of yourself
- Photos showing your living space (if offering a room)
- Property photos from authorized sources
- Non-explicit photos

**Unacceptable:**
- Nude or sexually explicit images
- Graphic violence
- Copyrighted images without permission
- Images of minors without appropriate context
- Stolen property photos
- Extreme violence or gore

## 5. Messaging Standards

### 5.1 Respectful Communication

Users should:
- Be respectful and courteous in all messages
- Assume good intent from others
- Engage in constructive dialogue
- Respectfully decline conversations

### 5.2 Prohibited Messaging Behavior

**Do Not:**
- Send unsolicited sexual or romantic advances
- Send repeatedly after a user indicates disinterest
- Threaten, harass, or abuse other users
- Share others' private information
- Use slurs, hate speech, or dehumanizing language
- Attempt to lure users outside the platform for scams
- Send messages with malicious links

### 5.3 First Contact Approach

**Best Practices:**
- Introduce yourself genuinely and mention why you're interested in connecting
- Be specific about your housing needs and timeline
- Ask genuine questions about their preferences or property
- Respect "no" responses and don't continue messaging

**Poor Approach:**
- Generic copy-paste messages
- Immediately requesting phone number or external contact
- Sexual or inappropriate opening messages
- Aggressive or demanding tone

## 6. Housing Transaction Guidelines

### 6.1 Honest Property Listings

If you're offering a room, property, or service:

**Do:**
- Provide accurate property details (size, amenities, condition)
- Include recent, accurate photos from multiple angles
- Clearly state rent, included utilities, and lease terms
- Be transparent about location and neighborhood
- Disclose any issues, renovations, or special requirements
- Clearly state move-in date and lease duration

**Don't:**
- Misrepresent property size or condition
- Hide defects, damage, or pest infestations
- Post photos from Google Images or other sources
- Claim amenities that don't exist
- Bait-and-switch with different prices
- Hide the actual location
- Post fake listings

### 6.2 Verification

- Meet potential roommates in person in public spaces before committing
- Verify employment and financial stability
- Request references from previous landlords or roommates
- Check background (optional, where legally permitted)
- Never share personal financial details via messaging
- Verify property ownership or landlord authorization

### 6.3 Safe Transaction Practices

- Never transfer money before viewing the property and meeting in person
- Use secure payment methods with buyer/renter protection
- Review lease agreements carefully
- Consider using escrow services for deposits
- Document everything in writing
- Report suspicious listings or scams immediately

### 6.4 Account Verification

RoomBud may introduce optional verification methods to improve trust and safety, including:
- Email verification
- Phone verification
- Identity verification
- Property verification

Verification may be used to confirm user identity, improve roommate matching, or validate listed properties. Participation may be optional unless required by local rules or specific service features.

## 7. Sensitive Topics

### 7.1 Acceptable Preferences

Users may state preferences for:
- Budget range
- Location and neighborhood
- Noise level and lifestyle
- Pet policies
- Smoking and drinking policies
- Working hours or sleep schedules

### 7.2 Problematic Preference Statements

Users should NOT:
- State gender-based exclusions in discriminatory or unlawful ways. Gender preferences related to shared accommodation may be stated when relevant to housing arrangements and where permitted by law.
- Use coded language for discrimination
- Demand medical or disability information
- Require specific religious or cultural practices in a way that is hateful or degrading
- Exclude based on protected characteristics
- Use sexually charged or inappropriate language

**Example:**
- **Acceptable:** "Looking for a non-smoker who respects quiet hours after 10pm"
- **Acceptable:** "Female roommate preferred for existing female-only flat"
- **Unacceptable:** "No [slur]. No [protected group]. No disabled people."

## 8. Reporting and Enforcement

### 8.1 How to Report Violations

If you encounter content or behavior violating these guidelines:

1. **In-App Report:** Click the Report button on the user's profile or message
2. **Select Category:** Choose the type of violation
3. **Provide Details:** Describe what happened and when
4. **Submit:** Attach screenshots if available

**Alternative:**
- Email: moderation@roombud.app
- Include: Offending user's profile link, description, and evidence

### 8.2 Investigation Process

- Reports are reviewed as promptly as reasonably possible, typically within 48 hours
- Investigations may include reviewing messages, profile content, and user history
- Context is considered; not all reports result in action
- Users are not notified of report actions unless the report is substantiated
- Moderators may contact users for additional information

### 8.3 Enforcement Actions

RoomBud may remove content, restrict visibility, suspend accounts, or cooperate with law enforcement when required by applicable law, court order, government directive, or user safety concerns.

Violations may result in one or more of the following:

**Level 1 - Content Removal:**
- Removal of specific content (message, photo, profile text)
- No account action
- User is notified

**Level 2 - Warning:**
- Formal warning to user
- Explanation of violation
- Notification that further violations may result in suspension

**Level 3 - Temporary Suspension:**
- Account disabled for 7-30 days
- Unable to message, browse, or modify profile
- User receives explanation of violation

**Level 4 - Permanent Ban:**
- Account permanently deleted
- RoomBud may use technical measures to prevent repeat abuse
- User information retained for legal purposes
- User barred from creating new accounts

### 8.4 Repeated Violations

Users with multiple violations face escalating consequences:
- First violation: Warning or content removal
- Second violation: Temporary suspension
- Third violation: Permanent ban

Serious violations (child safety, extreme hate speech, violence) may result in immediate permanent ban.

### 8.5 Appeals

Users may appeal enforcement actions:

1. **Contact:** moderation@roombud.app with subject "Appeal: [Your Account Email]"
2. **Provide:** Explanation of why you believe the action was unjust
3. **Timeline:** Appeals reviewed within 7 days
4. **Decision:** Final and binding
