# RoomBud - Data Deletion Policy

**Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Data Deletion Policy outlines how Roombud Technologies Private Limited handles user data deletion requests and the data retention practices we follow for the RoomBud product. We respect user privacy and provide options to delete, modify, or export your personal information.

This policy complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), and is intended to work together with our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) and [Terms of Service](TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md).

---

## 2. Your Data Deletion Rights

### 2.1 Right to Delete Your Account

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Upon verification of your identity, we will:

- Deactivate your account
- Remove your profile from public view
- Delete personal identifying information
- Disconnect messages from your identity
- Remove your data from most systems
- Permanently delete within specified timeframes

### 2.2 Right to Erasure

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. This includes deletion requests triggered by withdrawal of consent, the end of the processing purpose, or other lawful grounds.

Your request will be honored unless:

- Legal obligations require retention
- Ongoing investigations require preservation
- Legitimate legal defenses require records
- Service improvement research requires anonymized data

### 2.3 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format:

- All personal data you provided
- Your messages (where technically feasible)
- Your preferences and settings
- Your activity history
- In a common format (CSV, JSON)

### 2.4 Right to Correction

You can update or correct your personal information at any time:

- Edit profile information
- Update contact details
- Change preferences and settings
- Correct inaccuracies
- Add missing information

## 3. Account Deletion Process

### 3.1 How to Request Account Deletion

**Option 1: In-App Deletion**

1. Open RoomBud mobile app
2. Go to Settings
3. Select "Account"
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm deletion request
6. Enter password to verify
7. Select reason (optional)
8. Confirm final deletion

**Option 2: Web Dashboard**

1. Log into RoomBud website
2. Go to Account Settings
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm request
5. Enter password
6. Select reason (optional)
7. Confirm deletion

**Option 3: Email Request**

Send email to:
- **To:** privacy@roombud.app
- **Subject:** "Account Deletion Request"
- **Include:**
  - Your full name
  - Email address associated with account
  - Reason for deletion (optional)
  - Confirmation that you want all data deleted

### 3.2 Verification Process

Before deleting your account, RoomBud verifies your identity:

- **Email Verification:** Confirmation link sent to registered email
- **Password Verification:** Enter account password
- **Security Questions:** Answer security questions (if set up)
- **Phone Verification:** SMS code (if 2FA enabled)

**Why Verification is Important:**
- Prevents unauthorized deletions
- Protects your account from unauthorized requests
- Ensures intentional deletion
- Complies with legal requirements

### 3.3 Deletion Timeline

We will acknowledge deletion requests and respond within 30 days. We will complete erasure as soon as reasonably possible, subject to legal retention requirements.

**Immediate Actions (within 24 hours):**
- Account deactivated
- Profile hidden from search and browse
- Message history removed from senders' view
- Messaging capability disabled
- Login disabled

**Short-Term Deletion (within 7 days):**
- Personal identifying information removed
- Contact information deleted
- Location data deleted
- Preference data deleted
- Profile photos removed
- Bio and descriptions removed

**Extended Retention (30-90 days):**
- Transaction records retained (if applicable)
- Moderation records retained (if relevant)
- Security logs retained
- Payment records retained (for refund purposes)

**Final Deletion (90 days):**
- All remaining personal data permanently deleted
- Backup copies purged as soon as reasonably practicable
- Archive records deleted
- Aggregate statistics anonymized

**Exceptions:** Some data retained longer for legal/compliance reasons.

## 4. What Data Is Deleted

### 4.1 Data Deleted Upon Account Deletion

When you delete your account, the following data is deleted:

**Immediately:**
- Account credentials (email, password hash)
- Profile photos and images
- Personal information (name, age, date of birth, location)
- Contact information (email, phone)
- Bio and description
- Preferences and settings
- Payment methods (credit cards, etc.)
- Theme preferences
- Device information

**Within 7 Days:**
- Conversation history with other users
- Message content
- Search history
- Browsing history
- Activity logs
- Notification preferences
- Saved favorites

**Within 30-90 Days:**
- Backup copies of the above data
- Archive records
- Redundant copies

### 4.2 Data NOT Deleted or Partially Retained

**Messages from Other Users:** 
- Messages you received from other users remain in their conversation threads
- Your name is removed but message content remains
- Timestamps and context remain
- This allows other users to maintain their message history

**Transaction Records:**
- Payment history retained for 7 years (tax/legal requirements)
- Receipts and invoices retained
- Refund records retained
- Anonymized for privacy

**Legal/Compliance Records:**
- Records of Terms of Service violations
- Moderation decisions
- Safety investigations
- Law enforcement requests
- Litigation records
- Regulatory compliance files

**Anonymized/Aggregate Data:**
- Statistical data
- Research data
- Trend analysis
- De-identified usage patterns
- Cannot identify you individually

**Data Required by Law:**
- Certain jurisdictions require retention
- Tax records (7 years minimum in many places)
- Anti-money laundering records
- Government requests
- Court-ordered retention

**Withdrawal of Consent:**
- Upon withdrawal of consent, we will erase your personal data unless retention is required by law or for a valid legal purpose.

## 5. Data Retention Policies

### 5.1 Active Account Retention

**While your account is active, we retain:**
- All your profile information
- All messages and conversations
- Activity history
- Device information
- Login history
- Payment information

### 5.2 Inactive Account Retention

**Accounts inactive for 12+ months:**
- We may send notification of impending deletion
- Profile remains unless you delete it
- Data remains in systems
- 30-day notice period given before deletion
- If deletion is triggered by inactivity, we will provide at least 48 hours notice before automated deletion
- You can reactivate to prevent deletion

### 5.3 Deleted Account Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| Personal Information | 30-90 days | System purging |
| Transaction Records | 7 years | Legal/Tax |
| Moderation Records | 2 years | Legal/Compliance |
| Security Logs | 1 year | Security and compliance |
| Processing and Traffic Logs | 1 year | Compliance and investigation |
| Backup Copies | 90 days | System recovery |
| Law Enforcement Records | As required | Legal |
| Anonymized Data | Indefinite | Analytics/Research |

### 5.4 Backup and Disaster Recovery

**Why We Keep Backups:**
- System failure recovery
- Disaster recovery
- Data integrity
- Security purposes
- Legal compliance

**Backup Retention:**
- Full backups: 30 days
- Incremental backups: 7 days
- Archive backups: 90 days
- After deletion: Purged from all backups within 90 days

## 6. Special Deletion Scenarios

### 6.1 Data Subject to Court Orders

If a court orders data retention:
- We comply with legal orders
- Data retained as ordered
- You're notified (unless legally prohibited)
- Data deleted when order expires
- We can't delete if legal hold is active

### 6.2 Fraud Investigations

If your account is involved in fraud:
- Data retained during investigation
- Retained beyond 90 days if necessary
- Evidence preserved for law enforcement
- Retained if other users harmed
- Deleted after investigation resolves

### 6.3 Active Disputes or Complaints

Data retention during disputes:
- Retained while dispute is ongoing
- Used to resolve disagreement
- Deleted after resolution (if legally permitted)
- Kept longer if appeal is filed
- You're notified of retention

## 11. Permanent vs. Reversible Deletion

### 11.1 Account Deactivation

**Reversible (30 days):**
- Account hidden but not deleted
- Data remains on servers
- Can be reactivated
- 30-day recovery window

### 11.2 Account Deletion

**Permanent (after 30 days):**
- Cannot be recovered
- Data permanently deleted
- No recovery option
- Creates new account needed

### 11.3 Data Export vs. Deletion

**Data Export:**
- You receive a copy
- Data remains on RoomBud servers
- Separate from deletion
- Can still delete after export

**Deletion:**
- Data removed from RoomBud servers
- No personal copy kept by RoomBud
- Permanent after 30 days
- Cannot recover
