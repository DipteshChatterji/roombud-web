# RoomBud - Content Moderation Policy

**Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Content Moderation Policy outlines how Roombud Technologies Private Limited moderates content on RoomBud, identifies violations of our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service, and takes action where required. Our goal is to maintain a safe, respectful platform while respecting user freedom of expression.

---

## 2. Scope of Moderation

### 2.1 Content Subject to Moderation

The following types of content are subject to moderation:

- **User Profiles:**
  - Profile photos and images
  - Name and identifying information
  - Bio and description text
  - Preferences and stated requirements

- **Messages and Communications:**
  - Private messages between users
  - Conversation threads
  - Message content and attached links

**Note:** Private messages may be processed, reviewed, or investigated for safety, fraud prevention, and policy enforcement.

- **User-Generated Content:**
  - Property descriptions and listings
  - Reviews and ratings
  - Comments and feedback
  - Tags and hashtags

- **Account Activity:**
  - Behavioral patterns (spamming, manipulation)
  - Suspicious login attempts
  - Multiple fake accounts
  - Coordinated inauthentic behavior

### 2.2 Content NOT Subject to Moderation

The following are generally not moderated unless they violate specific policies:

- Differing opinions on roommate preferences (within legal bounds)
- Legitimate criticism of the platform
- Negative experiences (bad roommate, property issues)
- Requests for user assistance

---

## 3. Moderation Standards

### 3.1 Policy Hierarchy

Content is evaluated against:

1. **Illegal Content:** Indian law and applicable local regulations
2. **Safety Issues:** Child exploitation, violence, trafficking
3. **Community Standards:** Hate speech, harassment, discrimination
4. **Platform Rules:** Spam, fraud, misleading content
5. **User Preferences:** Not subject to removal

### 3.2 Context Consideration

Moderators consider context when reviewing content:

- **Purpose:** Is the content meant to harm or inform?
- **Audience:** Who is this content directed toward?
- **Intent:** Is the user attempting to violate policy?
- **Repetition:** Is this a pattern or isolated incident?
- **Recency:** When did the violation occur?

**Example of Context:**
- "I don't want to live with smokers" is acceptable
- "I won't live with [slur]" is a violation
- Discussing smoking as a topic is acceptable
- Using smoking as coded language for discrimination is not

### 3.3 Presumption of Good Faith

- We assume users are acting in good faith unless evidence suggests otherwise
- Mistakes, typos, and misunderstandings are handled gently
- Warnings precede most enforcement actions
- Final determination considers all available information

---

## 4. Moderation Methods

### 4.1 Automated Detection

RoomBud uses automated systems to:

- **Keyword Filtering:** Detect known slurs, hate speech, and illegal content
- **Behavioral Analysis:** Identify spam, fake profiles, and manipulation
- **Image Analysis:** Screen for explicit, violent, or child-unsafe content
- **Link Scanning:** Identify phishing, malware, and suspicious domains
- **Duplicate Detection:** Find fake profiles and coordinated inauthenticity

**Important:** Automated systems are not perfect and may:
- Generate false positives (flag legitimate content)
- Miss violations that humans would catch
- Require human review for context

### 4.2 Human Review

RoomBud employs trained moderators to:

- Review flagged content from automated systems
- Investigate user reports and complaints
- Evaluate context and intent
- Make final enforcement decisions
- Handle complex or ambiguous cases
- Monitor for new types of violations

### 4.3 User Reports

Users can report content by:

1. **In-App Report Button:** On profiles, messages, or listings
2. **Email:** moderation@roombud.app
3. **Support Tickets:** Through the help center
4. **Emergency:** safety@roombud.app (for immediate safety concerns)

Report categories include:
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Harassment or bullying
- Sexual harassment or exploitation
- Child safety concerns
- Fraud or misinformation
- Privacy violations
- Illegal activity
- Spam
- Other

## 5. Decision-Making Framework

### 5.1 Moderation Questions

When reviewing content, moderators ask:

1. **Is it Legal?**
   - Does the content violate local laws?
   - Is it child exploitation, terrorism, or violence?

2. **Is it Safe?**
   - Does it promote harm or violence?
   - Could it endanger users or minors?

3. **Is it Honest?**
   - Is it factually accurate?
   - Is it fraudulent or deceptive?
   - Does it violate intellectual property?

4. **Is it Respectful?**
   - Does it harass, bully, or abuse?
   - Does it discriminate based on protected characteristics?
   - Is it sexually exploitative?

5. **Is it Authentic?**
   - Is it spam or manipulation?
   - Is it from a fake or coordinated account?

### 5.2 Violations and Responses

| Violation Type | Initial Response | Second Violation | Third Violation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mild spam | Content removal | Warning + removal | 7-day suspension |
| Misleading content | Fact-check label + context | Content removal | Warning + removal |
| Profanity | Content warning | Content removal | 3-day suspension |
| Harassment | Warning | 3-day suspension | 7-day suspension |
| Sexual harassment | 3-day suspension | 7-day suspension | Permanent ban |
| Hate speech | 3-day suspension | 7-day suspension | Permanent ban |
| Child safety | Immediate permanent ban | N/A | N/A |

## 6. Specific Content Categories

### 6.1 Profile Photos

**Acceptable:**
- Clear, recent photos of your face
- Casual, natural photos
- Professional headshots
- Photos showing lifestyle or interests
- Photos of your living space (if offering a room)

**Unacceptable:**
- Nude or sexually explicit photos
- Photos of others (celebrities, strangers, partners without consent)
- Extreme violence or gore
- Graphic sexual imagery
- Copyrighted photos without permission
- Any photos of minors (absolutely prohibited)
- Heavily distorted or completely obscured faces (exception: privacy concerns)

**Action:** First offense = warning + removal of photo

### 6.2 Profile Text

**Acceptable:**
- Honest personal description
- Lifestyle preferences and habits
- What you're looking for in a roommate
- Interests and hobbies
- Reasonable preferences

**Unacceptable:**
- Slurs or hate speech
- Extreme vulgarity or sexual content
- Discriminatory language
- Illegal activity solicitation
- Spam or advertising
- Contact information

**Action:** First offense = content removal + warning

### 6.3 Messages

**Acceptable:**
- Genuine inquiries about property or preferences
- Personal conversation
- Scheduling meetings
- Sharing information (respectfully)
- Declining conversations

**Unacceptable:**
- Sexual harassment
- Repeated unwanted contact
- Threats or abuse
- Spam messages
- Phishing or scam attempts
- Attempts to move conversation off-platform for fraud

**Action:** Depends on severity (warning to suspension to ban)

### 6.4 Property Listings

**Acceptable:**
- Accurate property descriptions
- Current photos of the actual property
- Clear pricing and terms
- Real amenities list
- Honest disclosure of issues or requirements

**Unacceptable:**
- Photos from online sources (not your property)
- Inflated or deflated prices
- Fake amenities
- Hidden defects or damage
- Scam listings (non-existent property)
- Bait-and-switch descriptions

**Action:** Content removal + warning; repeated = suspension

## 7. Sensitive Content Handling

### 7.1 Marginalized Communities

Content affecting marginalized communities receives special attention:

- **Review Priority:** Prioritized for immediate human review
- **Expert Input:** Reviewed by moderators from affected communities
- **Bias Training:** Moderators trained on cultural sensitivity
- **Context:** Heavily weighted toward context and good faith

### 7.2 Mental Health and Self-Harm

- Content promoting self-harm or suicide is prohibited
- Resources are provided to struggling users
- Emergency contacts are shown for crisis situations
- User safety is prioritized over platform engagement

### 7.3 Health and Medical Misinformation

- Medical advice is not permitted in profiles
- Discriminatory health-based exclusions may be flagged
- RoomBud does not verify health claims made by users

## 8. Appeals Process

### 8.1 Appealing Content Removal

If your content is removed:

1. **Notification:** You receive explanation of removal
2. **Appeal Window:** 30 days to appeal
3. **Process:**
   - Email moderation@roombud.app
   - Subject: "Appeal: Content Removal"
   - Include: Content description, reasons why it should not be removed
4. **Review:** Independent moderator reviews appeal
5. **Decision:** Response within 7 days
6. **Options:** Content restored or removal upheld

### 8.2 Appealing Account Actions

If your account receives a warning or suspension:

1. **Notification:** Email with explanation and appeals deadline
2. **Grounds for Appeal:**
   - Mistaken identity
   - Misunderstanding of policy
   - Disproportionate punishment
   - New evidence of innocence
3. **Process:** Email moderation@roombud.app within 30 days
4. **Review:** Senior moderator reviews case
5. **Timeline:** Decision within 14 days

### 8.3 Appeal Outcomes

- **Appeal Granted:** Action reversed or reduced
- **Appeal Denied:** Original action upheld
- **Appeal Partially Granted:** Modified action (e.g., shorter suspension)
- **Final:** All appeals decisions are final

- Peer support groups
- Supervisor check-ins

### 9.4 Moderator Accountability

- Random audits of decisions
- User appeals of moderator actions
- Complaint process for problematic moderators

## 10. Data Retention for Moderation

### 10.1 What We Keep

For enforcement purposes, we retain:
- Reported content (even if removed)
- User reports and investigation records
- Moderator notes and decisions
- Patterns of violation history
- Account action records

### 10.2 Retention Timeline

- **Active Cases:** Retained during investigation
- **Resolved Cases:** Retained for 1 year
- **Archived Cases:** Retained for 2 years (legal/compliance)
- **Deletion:** Permanently deleted after retention period

### 10.3 Transparency

Users can request:
- Why content was removed
- An explanation of moderation actions
- History of actions taken
- Evidence used in decision (where legally permissible)
