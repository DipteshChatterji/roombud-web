# RoomBud - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Roombud Technologies Private Limited ("Roombud Technologies," "we," "us," "our," "Company," or "Platform") operates RoomBud, a product designed to connect users seeking roommates and rental accommodations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use RoomBud.

For users in India, personal data is processed in accordance with applicable Indian data protection laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

**Account Registration:**
- Email address (used for account creation and verification)
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Phone number (optional)
- Profile picture(s) and images
- Gender and gender preference
- Sexual orientation or relationship status (optional)

**Profile Information:**
- Age
- City, state, and area/locality
- Profession or employment status
- Budget (minimum and maximum rent, currency)
- Moving date
- Bio/personal description (up to 500 characters)
- Preferences: smoking, drinking, pets, food preferences, hygiene standards, sleep schedule
- Languages spoken
- Religion or spiritual preferences
- Disabilities or accessibility requirements
- Whether you have a room to share (room availability)

These fields are optional. Users may choose not to provide them. We collect this information solely to improve roommate compatibility, preference matching, and overall user experience.

**Content You Create:**
- Messages and conversations with other Users
- Favorite profiles or rooms
- Blocks and reports
- Theme preference (app display theme)

**Payment and Billing Information:**
- If applicable: payment method details (processed through secure third-party providers)
- Billing address
- Transaction history

**Communications:**
- Feedback, surveys, and support tickets
- Email communications and newsletters

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type, model, and operating system
- Device identifier (UDID) or advertising identifier
- Browser type and version
- Mobile device information

**Usage Information:**
- Pages or features accessed
- Time and duration of visits
- Actions performed (profile views, messages sent, blocks, favorites)
- Search queries and filters used
- App crash reports and performance data

**Location Information:**
- City-level location data from your profile
- Approximate location based on IP address (if permissions granted)
- GPS location (only if you explicitly enable location permissions)

**Authentication Data:**
- Supabase authentication tokens
- Login history and authentication methods (email, Google OAuth)
- Session information

**Cookies and Tracking Technology:**
- Cookies to maintain session state
- Web beacons and pixel tags for analytics
- Analytics data for service improvement and crash reporting

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

**Google OAuth:**
- If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your Google profile data (name, email, profile picture)
- Google ID and OAuth tokens

**Supabase:**
- Authentication and verification data

**Communication Platforms:**
- If you share RoomBud referral links, we may receive information about shared content

## 5. How We Share Your Information

### 5.1 User-to-User Sharing

When you use RoomBud, the following information is shared with other Users:

- **Public Profile:** Name, age, city, profession, profile picture(s), gender, preferences, bio
- **Matching Information:** Information relevant to roommate/room matching
- **In Your Browsing:** Your profile appears in search results and browse recommendations
- **Messages:** Your messages are shared with the recipient only

**Note:** Email addresses are NOT shared with other Users unless you choose to share them directly.

### 5.2 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who assist in operating the Service. These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually required to follow our instructions and protect your information.

- **Supabase:** Authentication, PostgreSQL database services, and real-time backend infrastructure
- **Render:** Hosting and application deployment
- **Firebase:** Notifications, optional analytics, and support services
- **SiteEngine:** Web hosting and site delivery
- **GeoAPIFI:** Geolocation and mapping services
- **Google Cloud:** Infrastructure and hosting
- **Email Service Providers:** Email delivery
- **Customer Support Tools:** Support ticketing and communication

We require our providers to process data only to provide services to RoomBud and to comply with applicable Indian data protection requirements.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or when:
- Compelled by court order, subpoena, or legal process
- Necessary to protect public safety or prevent harm
- Investigating or preventing suspected fraud or illegal activity
- Protecting our legal rights and intellectual property
- Responding to government requests or national security matters

### 5.4 Business Transfers

If RoomBud is acquired, merged, or substantially reorganized, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of such changes via email or prominent notice.

### 5.5 Aggregated and De-Identified Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated data (statistics, trends, research) with:
- Research institutions
- Marketing partners
- Data analytics providers
- Third parties for benchmarking

This data cannot identify you personally.

### 5.6 With Your Consent

We may share your information for purposes other than those listed above when you provide explicit consent.

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Retention Period

- **Active Accounts:** Data is retained for the duration of your account
- **Inactive Accounts:** Accounts inactive for 12 months may be subject to deletion
- **Messages:** Conversation history is retained while accounts remain active and may be retained for a limited period afterward if required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or abuse prevention
- **Analytics Data:** Anonymized analytics retained for 24 months
- **Logs:** System and security logs retained for 90 days
- **Legal Hold:** Data retained longer if required by legal proceedings

### 6.2 Account Deletion

Upon your request to delete your account:
- Your profile becomes inaccessible
- Your name and most personal data are removed
- Messages remain in conversations with other Users
- Anonymized data may be retained for analytics
- Some data may be retained for legal or compliance purposes

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption in Transit:** SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
- **Encryption at Rest:** Database encryption for sensitive data
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access; only authorized staff access personal data
- **Authentication:** Supabase secure authentication with access tokens
- **Password Policy:** We do not store plain-text passwords; they are hashed and salted
- **Access Controls:** We limit access to personal data to authorized personnel only
- **Monitoring:** We monitor systems for suspicious activity and enforce security controls
- **Employee Training:** Staff trained on data privacy and security

### 7.2 Limitations

While we maintain strong security practices, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You use the Service at your own risk.

### 7.3 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:
- Notify you via email
- Disclose the nature of the breach
- Provide recommended steps to protect your information
- Comply with relevant data protection laws

## 8. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### 8.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Receive your data in a structured, commonly-used format
- Request a copy of your complete profile and data

**To exercise:** Email support@roombud.app with "Data Access Request"

### 8.2 Correction

You may update or correct your information directly in your account settings at any time.

### 8.3 Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data. Upon confirmation, we will:
- Delete your profile
- Remove your personal identifying information
- Retain anonymized data for analytics and legal purposes

**To exercise:** Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account

### 8.4 Opt-Out of Communications

- **Marketing Emails:** Click "Unsubscribe" in email footers
- **Push Notifications:** Disable in app settings
- **In-App Notifications:** Manage notification preferences
- **Analytics Tracking:** Use opt-out mechanisms in your browser

Note: You cannot opt out of transactional or legally required communications.

### 8.5 Cookies and Tracking

- Clear cookies in your browser settings
- Use browser "Do Not Track" signals (we honor this where applicable)
- Disable location permissions in device settings

### 8.6 Indian Privacy Rights

RoomBud respects the privacy rights of individuals under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

- **Right to access:** Request confirmation of the personal data we hold about you
- **Right to correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure:** Request deletion of personal data when retention is not required by law
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Withdraw consent for processing that is based on consent
- **Right to grievance redressal:** Submit a complaint to our grievance officer for unresolved privacy issues

We will respond to verified requests in accordance with applicable Indian law. Our deletion process is described in the Data Deletion Policy.

## 9. Age Verification

RoomBud is exclusively for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 18. If we discover that a user under 18 has created an account, we will:

- Immediately deactivate the account
- Delete all associated personal data
- Take reasonable steps to prevent re-registration using the same email address

If you believe an underage user has created an account, please contact support@roombud.app immediately.
