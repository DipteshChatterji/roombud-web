# RoomBud - Safety Policy

**Effective Date:** June 11, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Roombud Technologies Private Limited operates RoomBud and is committed to the safety and well-being of all users. This Safety Policy outlines the measures we take to protect users from harm, exploitation, harassment, and fraud. We also provide guidance to users on how to protect themselves while using our platform.

RoomBud processes personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), where applicable.

**Important Disclaimer:** While RoomBud implements safety measures, we cannot guarantee complete safety. Users are responsible for exercising judgment and taking precautions when interacting with other users.

## 2. RoomBud's Safety Responsibilities

RoomBud implements:

- **Account Security:**
  - Secure authentication via Supabase
  - Password encryption and salting

  - Optional two-factor authentication (2FA)
  - Session management and timeout

- **Data Protection:**
  - SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  - Database encryption for data at rest
  - Regular security audits
  - Vulnerability testing
  - Firewalls

- **Infrastructure Security:**
  - Google Cloud secure hosting
  - Regular backups
  - Disaster recovery procedures
  - Network security

### 2.2 User Moderation

RoomBud:

- **Monitors for Harm:**
  - AI and human moderation of content

  - Investigation of safety complaints
  - Enforcement of Community Guidelines

- **Takes Action Against Bad Actors:**
  - Suspends dangerous accounts
  - Bans habitual offenders
  - Retains evidence when required by law

### 2.3 Reporting and Support

RoomBud provides:

- **Easy Reporting:** In-app report buttons
- **Multiple Channels:** Email, support portal, phone
- **Timely Response:** Reports are reviewed as promptly as reasonably possible, typically within 48 hours
- **Support Resources:** Links to resources and information
- **Legal Cooperation:** RoomBud may cooperate with law enforcement when required by applicable law or necessary to protect user safety

## 3. User Safety Responsibilities

### 3.1 Account Safety

You are responsible for:

- **Strong Passwords:**
  - Use unique, strong passwords
  - Never share passwords with others
  - Change passwords if compromised
  - Use different passwords across platforms

- **Email Security:**
  - Protect your email account
  - Don't access RoomBud on public Wi-Fi for account changes
  - Verify recovery email and phone number
  - Enable 2FA if available

- **Verification:**
  - Enable email verification
  - Use unique recovery methods
  - Keep contact information updated

### 3.2 Profile Safety

Best practices:

- **Photos:**
  - Use recent, authentic photos
  - Show your face clearly
  - Don't use photos of others
  - Avoid overly revealing images
  - Don't include sensitive information in photos

- **Personal Information:**
  - Don't post full address initially
  - Avoid workplace/school names
  - Don't list full phone number in profile
  - Keep exact location vague until trust established
  - Don't share financial information

### 3.3 Messaging Safety

When communicating:

- **Initial Contact:**
  - Be skeptical of generic messages
  - Don't engage with suspicious profiles
  - Block users who make you uncomfortable
  - Report harassment immediately
  - Don't respond to inappropriate messages

- **Information Sharing:**
  - Don't share phone number immediately
  - Don't share financial details
  - Don't send personal documents early
  - Don't share passwords or access codes
  - Don't send money before meeting

- **Red Flags:**
  - Rushed or pressured interactions
  - Requests for money or gift cards
  - Unwillingness to video call or meet
  - Inconsistent stories or details
  - Attempts to move conversation off-platform quickly

## 4. Hazardous Behavior Detection and Response

### 4.1 Suspicious Profile Patterns

RoomBud monitors for:

- **Fake Profiles:**
  - Recently created accounts with immediate suspicious activity
  - Stock photos or images of others
  - Inconsistent or vague information
  - Multiple profiles from same user
  - Rapid profile changes or updates

- **Predatory Behavior:**
  - Rapid messaging to many users
  - Sexual or inappropriate initial messages
  - Attempts to meet quickly without vetting
  - Consistent pattern of targeting vulnerable users
  - Attempts to move conversations off-platform

- **Fraud Patterns:**
  - Too-good-to-be-true property listings
  - Requests for deposits before viewing
  - Multiple similar listings from same user
  - Requests for money transfer
  - Inconsistent pricing or terms

### 4.2 Response Protocol

When suspicious activity is detected:

1. **Flag:** Content/account flagged for review
2. **Investigate:** Moderator reviews account history and patterns
3. **Risk Assessment:** Determine level and type of threat
4. **Action:** Warning, suspension, or ban
5. **Evidence Retention:** Evidence retained for law enforcement
6. **Notification:** Affected users notified if appropriate

## 5. Specific Safety Threats

### 5.1 Harassment and Abuse

**What It Is:**
- Repeated unwanted contact
- Threats or intimidation
- Cyberbullying or public humiliation
- Sexual harassment or advances
- Discrimination or hate speech

**How We Respond:**
- Blocking tools provided to users
- Escalation to suspension/ban
- RoomBud may cooperate with law enforcement when required by applicable law or necessary to protect user safety
- Support resources provided
- Evidence retained when required by law

**What Users Should Do:**
- Use the block feature
- Don't respond or engage
- Save evidence (screenshots)
- Report to moderation team
- Contact local authorities if threatened
- Use support resources

### 5.2 Sexual Exploitation and Abuse

**What It Is:**
- Sexual harassment or coercion
- Solicitation of sexual services
- Sharing intimate images without consent
- Sexual manipulation or grooming
- Child exploitation

**RoomBud's Response:**
- Zero tolerance policy
- Immediate permanent ban
- RoomBud may cooperate with law enforcement when required by applicable law or necessary to protect user safety
- Evidence retention and cooperation when required by law

**What Users Should Do:**
- Don't engage with inappropriate requests
- Block immediately
- Report to moderation
- Contact local authorities if child exploitation is suspected
- Use support resources

### 5.3 Fraud and Scams

**Common Types:**

| Scam Type | How It Works | Red Flags |
|-----------|------------|-----------|
| Advance-Fee Rental | Asks for deposit before seeing property | Pressure to pay quickly, unwillingness to meet in person |
| Wire Fraud | Uses stolen property photos, transfers funds | Property too cheap, professional photos, pressure to decide |
| Romance Scam | Builds trust then requests money | Moves fast emotionally, requests money for "emergency" |
| Identity Theft | Uses fake identity to fraud other users | Inconsistent stories, generic photos, vague information |
| Bait-and-Switch | Changes terms after agreement | Different property shown at viewing, different price than listed |

**RoomBud's Response:**
- Monitor for fraud patterns
- Fraudulent accounts suspended/banned
- Cooperation with authorities
- User notifications
- Reversal of fraudulent transactions (where possible)

**What Users Should Do:**
- Never transfer money before viewing in person
- Verify property ownership
- Meet in public places
- Trust your instincts
- Report suspicious activity
- Use secure payment methods with protection
- File police report if defrauded

### 5.4 Privacy Violations (Doxxing)

**What It Is:**

- Publishing address, phone, or email publicly

- Recording private conversations to shame

**RoomBud's Response:**

- Account investigation

- RoomBud may cooperate with law enforcement when required by applicable law or necessary to protect user safety

**What Users Should Do:**
- Use privacy settings
- Don't share personal information publicly
- Don't record conversations without consent
- Report violations immediately
- Contact law enforcement if threatened
- Document abuse

### 5.5 Discrimination and Hate Crimes

**What It Is:**

- Harassment targeting protected groups

**RoomBud's Response:**

**What Users Should Do:**

### 5.6 Housing Discrimination

Users may express lawful roommate compatibility preferences such as gender preference, dietary preference, language preference, or religious compatibility preference. Preferences must not be expressed using hateful, abusive, or degrading language.

**RoomBud's Response:**

**What Users Should Do:**

## 6. Meeting Safety Guidelines

### 6.1 Before Meeting

- **Verify Identity:** Video call before meeting
- **Share Plans:** Tell a friend where you're going
- **Background Check:** Use available tools to verify information
- **Trust Your Instincts:** If something feels off, cancel
- **Multiple Viewings:** Visit multiple properties before deciding
- **Review Carefully:** Inspect property thoroughly

### 6.2 Meeting Location and Timing

- **Public Spaces:** Always meet in public places (coffee shop, shopping center)
- **Daytime:** Meet during daylight hours
- **Bring Someone:** Bring a trusted friend or family member
- **Let Others Know:** Share location with trusted person
- **Avoid Isolation:** Never meet in private spaces alone
- **Transportation:** Have your own transportation

### 6.3 During the Meeting

- **Assessment:**
  - Assess the person and property
  - Note inconsistencies with profile/description
  - Check property condition thoroughly
  - Verify ownership/lease authorization
  - Verify utilities and services

- **Safety Awareness:**
  - Trust your instincts about the person
  - Notice if you feel pressured
  - Observe the neighborhood safety
  - Identify exits
  - Keep phone accessible

- **Documentation:**
  - Take photos/video of property
  - Get all terms in writing
  - Photograph any damage
  - Get contact information
  - Note date and time

### 6.4 Financial Safety

- **Never Transfer Money Before:**
  - Seeing the property in person
  - Meeting the landlord/roommate
  - Having a signed lease
  - Verifying ownership/authorization

- **Payment Methods:**
  - Use secure payment methods (credit card, bank transfer, cashier's check)
  - Avoid wire transfers, gift cards, cryptocurrency
  - Keep payment records and receipts
  - Use escrow services for deposits (when available)
  - Get written receipt for deposits

- **Lease Documents:**
  - Get lease in writing
  - Review all terms carefully
  - Understand your rights
  - Consider legal review
  - Consult tenant rights organizations
  - Keep copies of all documents

## 9. What To Do If Safety Issues Occur

### 9.1 Immediate Danger

**If in immediate danger:**
- Call 112 (National Emergency Number) or other local emergency services
- Move to a safe location
- Tell a trusted person
- Contact police
- Preserve evidence (photos, messages)
- Seek medical attention if needed

**Do NOT:**
- Confront the perpetrator
- Destroy evidence
- Handle alone

### 9.2 Scam or Fraud

**If you've been scammed:**

1. **Stop Communication:** Don't continue engaging
2. **Preserve Evidence:** Screenshot all communications
3. **Report to RoomBud:**
   - In-app report or email moderation@roombud.app
   - Include all evidence
4. **Report to Authorities:**
   - File a police report
   - Report to your local consumer protection agency
5. **Financial Recourse:**
   - Contact your bank/payment provider
   - Report fraud to payment processor
   - Request charge reversal (credit card)
   - File claim with escrow (if used)
6. **Follow Up:**
   - Keep copies of all reports
   - Update RoomBud if scammer creates new account
   - Monitor for identity theft

### 9.3 Harassment or Abuse

**If you're being harassed:**

1. **Document:** Screenshot all harassment
2. **Block:** Use block feature on RoomBud
3. **Report:** Report to RoomBud moderation
4. **Report to Law Enforcement:** If threats or ongoing harassment
5. **Seek Support:**
   - Contact support hotlines
   - Access available resources
   - Reach out to trusted friends/family
   - Use community resources

**Hotlines:**
- Emergency: 112 (National Emergency Number)
- Police: 100
- Women's Helpline: 1091
- Childline: 1098
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in

### 9.4 Discrimination or Hate

**If you've experienced discrimination:**

1. **Document:** Note date, time, what happened
2. **Report to RoomBud:** moderation@roombud.app
3. **File Civil Complaint:**
   - Local human rights commission or equivalent
   - Consumer protection agency if applicable
   - Police if the behavior is criminal
4. **Seek Legal Help:**
   - Consult an attorney
   - Know your rights in your jurisdiction
5. **Support:** Access community/identity-based support groups

### 9.5 Privacy Violation (Doxxing)

**If your private information was shared:**

1. **Report to RoomBud:** Request immediate removal
2. **Contact Platforms:** Request removal from social media, etc.
3. **Monitor:** Watch for misuse of information
4. **Change Security:** Change passwords, monitor accounts
5. **Report to Law Enforcement:** If threats or harassment follow
6. **Support:** Seek counseling if emotionally affected
